Our Objective

We are Sayli and Sai Priya, High School Seniors. We are developers, creators and keen learners. Through out our journey we always faced difficulties in many fields, and a lot of the time we had no one to guide us so it was really challenging to keep working. As we kept developing and learning we stumbled upon various websites and platforms that helped pave a path for us. After this, we thought why not help fellow students and learners with all the things we have come across? This resource-page helps find free and important resources in various fields! We hope that we can help fellow students, ;learners and innovators in many ways.
